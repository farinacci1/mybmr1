How it Works


Personalize Your Account

Go to you profile by clicking the icon on the left.

Let us know how much you want to spend per week and how many calories you want to consume per day!

We'll let you know when you're overbudget.


Discover Your New Favorite Recipes and Restaurants

Use our search feature to find great-tasting recipes.

Don't feel like cooking? You can also search restaurants that are in your price range.

If you find something you especially like, you can favorite it and find it again easily using the favorites icon on the left!


Add To Your Calendar

Make sure to connect to your calendar!

Pick the time and day you want to enjoy the meal or eat out, and add it to your meal plan.

When you add a meal to your plan, we create an event in your calendar to help you stay on track.
